Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Bavarian Formula, and why is it essential?

It is the official mathematical equation used by German universities to convert international grading scales into the German 1.0 to 4.0 system. Knowing this score tells you exactly which universities you are qualified to apply for.

Are public university master and bachelor programs truly free?

Yes. State-funded public higher education across Germany features zero tuition costs for both local and international scholars. Students pay only a nominal semester contribution fee for public transit and campus administrative upkeep.

What is the required blocked account amount?

To receive a student visa, German immigration requires proof of financial self-sufficiency through a regulated blocked account, currently configured to a secure reserve baseline of €11,904.
